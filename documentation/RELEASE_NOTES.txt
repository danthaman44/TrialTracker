Current functionality:
1. Users can register for accounts. Accounts are validated by email

2. Users can create new trials, and can specific when the trial began and ended. 
The creator of a trial has administrator privelidges over the trial. The creator
can modify the trial's settings, invite/remove people from the trial, and delete the trial if they choose

3. Users can add one data entry per day for their trials. Entry data includes the number
people enrolled, active, withdrawn, refused, and completed for a trial

4. Users can invite other users to join their trial by email

5. Users can see their entry data in a bar chart and a progress-over-time line graph.
The line chart allows users to see projected and target numbers as well

TrialTracker has been tested on the Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, and Apple Safari
browsers. It achieved satisfactory performace on each.
